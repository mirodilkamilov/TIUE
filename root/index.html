Our Vision

Tashkent International University of Education will become a purposeful education hub/corporation that will disrupt education and have a transformative impact on nations through promoting innovations, research, creativity, and entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an applied learning environment that allows all participants of the educational process to discover, examine critically, preserve and transmit the knowledge, wisdom and values that will help ensure the existence of this and future generations and improve the quality of life for all.

Our Values

Affordable education

Bachelor's degree

TIUE offers three faculties in the bachelor's degree:

  • Business Management

    Business management program provide a broad knowledge of the various business disciplines, such as accounting, marketing, insurance, finance, administration, management science and human resource management.

  • Information and Communication Subjects

    Information Communication Technology students gain knowledge and skills needed to effectively apply, use and manage technology when solving problems specifically related to information and communication.

  • Pedagogical Disciplines (including the STEAM Education)

    STEAM is an educational approach to learning that uses Science, Technology, Engineering, the Arts and Mathematics as access points for guiding student inquiry, dialogue, and critical thinking
