News and Events

Establishment of TIUE

A new educational institution was established in accordance with the Decree of the Cabinet of Ministers of the Republic of Uzbekistan "About the establishment of the international university "Tashkent International University of Education" No. 404 dated June 23, 2020. TIUE is granted the right to determine foreign higher education institutions ranked in TOP 1000 ranking of the Times Higher Education World University Rankings as international partners and establishing their branches on its base. Graduates of the University are awarded a diploma of international level or a standard sample of foreign partner universities, which is recognized as a document of higher education in Uzbekistan. University graduates are issued a diploma of international level or the established sample of foreign partner universities, which is recognized as a document of higher education in Uzbekistan as well.

New collaboration with ISASS

TIUE signs MOU with International School of Applied Social Sciences (ISASS), registered in Barcelona, Spain. ISASS provides diploma and degree courses in partnership with various educational institutions in Europe and Asia, including the Swiss school of management. The agreement aims to develop mutual scientific and technical cooperation in the provision of innovative educational programs and business development between two universities.

TSEU agreed to collaborate

Tashkent International University of Education and Tashkent State University of Economics signed the Agreement to develop the interaction between these two educational institutions with the aim of implementing projects in areas of mutual interest, such as developing educational programs in the field of business management, international relations, teaching language and other preparatory courses, developing the business ecosystem as well.

TIEU took part on International webinar

Tashkent International University of Education took an active part in the webinar which was organized on November 30, 2020 by the Department of International Trade of the British Embassy. The main purpose of the webinar was to introduce British universities to the opportunities for cooperation in Higher education and to establish international relations in Central Asian countries, in particular with Uzbekistan and Kazakhstan.

OEP continues collaboration with TIUE

International commercial arm of four Finnish organizations with outstanding expertise in education, training and development cooperation - Omnia Education Partnerships Ltd, And Tashkent International University of Education (TIUE) signed MOU to further develop the education system in Uzbekistan
